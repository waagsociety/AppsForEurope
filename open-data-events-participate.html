Join our network


The Apps for Europe consortium welcomes new members in our network, to share experiences, knowledge and help show the value of open data. See the current members of our network here. You can join our network in several ways.

Join our meetups

Apps for Europe partners meetup several times a year during large events like the Open Knowledge Festival, FutureEverything etc. Look at the events calendar to find the next meeting.

Letter of support

Do you support our goals? Let us know by signing our letter of support. This will help show the European Commission the importance of helping the open data community turn their ideas into viable business. Contact us for details.

Join the thematic network

Our consortium invites organizations involved in open data events, competitions and hackathons to join our network and engage with other members.  Contact us for details.
