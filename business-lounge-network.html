The Apps for Europe Business Lounge network

We are proud of the current members of our network and we invite you to join us. 

INVESTORS @ BUSINESS LOUNGES SO FAR

      

          

 

                                

 

Flanders DC
Flanders DC is a Flemish organization for entrepreneurial creativity commissioned by the Flemish Government. The Flanders DC Knowledge Centre studies trends in creativity and innovation and provides a scientific foundation for their projects.
http://www.flandersdc.be

De Investeerders Club
De Investeerders Club (The Investors Club) is an informal network of some 50 active investors in the Netherlands. In 2012, De Investeerders Club closed 17 deals with a total value of more than €1.7 million.
http://www.deinvesteerdersclub.nl

Peak Capital
Peak Capital is an independent Dutch investment company focused on seed and early stage companies in the Telecom, Media and Technology (TMT) sector. Peak Capital was founded in 2007 and currently has two funds with a combined size of €6 million.
http://www.peakcapital.nl

Startupbootcamp
Startupbootcamp is a 3 month acceleration program that focuses on exposing and connecting startups to an expanding community of top-level mentors and advisors in Europe. On average over 70% of the graduates have gone on to receive funding.
http://www.startupbootcamp.org

Future Plus
Future Plus was founded in 2010 by a small group of Spanish technological executives and entrepreneurs, with a clear and precise goal: driving technological innovation in Spain and related countries (Latam and Med areas).

To fulfill this goal Future Plus is acting in two main axes:

· For new projects or SMEs, through a Venture Capital fund that Future Plus co-created and manage: AMBAR.
· For large projects and well established corporations or institutions, as very specialized advisors and consultants.
http://www.futureplus.es

 

OTHER MEMBERS OF THE APPS for EUROPE NETWORK 

 
 

                                                                         

OpenKratio is an organisation of commited citizens advocating for spreading the principles of #opengovernment and #opendata in Spain.

Nowadays OpenKratio is contributing to the future Transparency and Citizenship Participation Regional Laws for Andalusia, and coordinating Proyecto Colibrí: a parliamentary monitoring initiative. OpenKratio is a host of  annual national summit about #opendata and #opengovernment. The next event will take place on 21st of February, 2014. http://openkratio.org
