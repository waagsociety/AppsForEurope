Funding opportunities in the EU


Europe currently is offering many great funding opportunities for start ups and SME's focussing on technology and innovation. We will update this page regularly with new information. If you know of any funding tools that are not included, please contact us at info@localhost/apps.

FI-WARE - 80 million euros

FIWARE seeks to provide a truly open, public and royalty-free architecture and a set of open specifications that will allow developers, service providers, enterprises and other organizations to develop products that satisfy their needs while still being open and innovative. They offer many easy accessible funding opportunities to connect to their platform. View all calls at: http://www.fi-ware.org/accelerators.

Important upcoming deadline: 31st of October
