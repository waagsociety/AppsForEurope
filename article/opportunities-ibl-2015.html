Opportunities IBL 2015

The second Apps for Europe IBL (international business lounge) will take place during the FutureEverything conference on 26 and 27 February 2015 in Manchester (UK). The finalists from the annual online competition and the local business lounges in 2014 will travel to Manchester and pitch their project in front of international investors and experts.

Join us at FutureEverything

At FutureEverything we offer many networking opportunities and the chance to meet the most interesting open data startups in Europe. Participation is limited, so let us know if you want to join. Please send an email to: ivonne@waag.org/apps
 
Winners local Business Lounges
The international business lounge will be host to all the winners of local Apps for Europe Business Lounges throughout Europe. The current startups are confirmed. The list will be expanded in the coming months as more business lounges will happen.
 
                                                      
 

Advisory Board
The Apps for Europe advisory Board will be part of the international business lounge:
                 
              
   Marleen Stikker, president Waag Society
   David Eaves, open government advisor
   Peter Corbett, CEO iStrategyLabs
   Miquel Costa, chairman Keiretsu Forum
   Richard Stirling, international director Open Data Insitute
   Nigel Jacob, Boston Mayors office

Sponsorship

Are you interested to be connected to the Apps for Europe network? We are currently looking for sponsors to contribute to the international business lounge. Contact us for more information: ivonne@waag.org
