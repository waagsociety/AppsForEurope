Join our investor network


Apps for Europe invites you to be part of our investor network. The participants of the network will be connected to selected developers, start-ups and entrepreneurs who have a business idea, working prototype or are already running their own business based on open data. The first selection of these businesses will be done by the partners of the Apps for Europe consortium that are organizing open data events, competitions or hackathons. They have been active in the open data community for many years and have a good overview of individuals and team creating innovative services. Have a look at the current network here. 

Contact us for more information on joining the investor network

Why join us

Participating investors get the chance to select projects by maturity level, area of activity and growth potential. Apps for Europe provides metrics defined for the Business Lounge. We are looking for investors specialized in investing in different sectors, range of financing, territories. But also potential clients and organizations looking to support open data start-ups are more than welcome to join.

The Apps for Europe network provides one on one support. We customize involvement in order to be aligned with your current activity.

  • Innovative projects to invest in 
    Investors get a great chance to meet the most innovative and successful new entrepreneurs.
  • Pre-filtered projects
    Investors have a chance to browse already pre-filtered projects by the idea, its maturity, location and other parameters.
  • Direct contact with the event organizers
    Members of the investors network can get in direct contact with event organizers, facilitating the process of browsing potential start-ups that fit their interests.
  • Cost-free joining to the network
    Joining the network is free of charge and doesn't require any commitment to invest.
  • Promotion
    Participating in the investors network offers a chance to place your corporate image in all Apps4Europe publicity activities, including both offline and online.
  • Corporate Social Responsibility
    Investing in open data Apps is a great way to support your community. It is a chance to improve quality of life, offering better public services and more transparent government.
  • Networking and enjoyable interaction with a peer group
    Investors get a chance for networking, exchanging ideas with leaders around Europe.
