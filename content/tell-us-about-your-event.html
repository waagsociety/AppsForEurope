Tell us about your event

Add a Business Lounge to your event

Adding a Business Lounge to your event can help open data developers take the next step in turning their idea into viable business. But is also means that you as an organizer need to take that extra step to help them connect to the right people. Apps for Europe can support your effort with ready made formats and a network of investors to connect to. If you are interested in the idea of the Business Lounge, please contact us so we can help you add one to your event.
